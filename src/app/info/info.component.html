HOW TO PLAY

Baduk Guessr allows you to study professional games of Go in a fun way, trying to get the highest score possible. In every move, pick one (A) or more moves (up to D) as potential next moves. Then, click "guess" (or press ENTER). If one of your guesses is correct, you earn points. The less options you pick, the most points you get if you are correct. Alternatively you can use the arrow keys or the move buttons in order to navigate the game without guessing. Be warned however that if you see a move this way you won't be allowed to earn points from guessing it later.

Every {{showScoreFrequency}} moves (or when the end of the game is reached) your score history will be updated and a popup will appear with your score progress so far. You can also access this score progress by clicking on the total score displayed on top of the board. Click COPY in order to copy your total score in your clipboard and share it with your friends.

Additionally at the end of the game you will have the option of downloading an sgf file with the daily game or, alternatively, re-start the game and give it a second go.

Have fun!
