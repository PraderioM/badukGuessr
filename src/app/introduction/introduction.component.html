HOW TO PLAY

Baduk Guessr allows you to study professional games of Go in a fun way, trying to get the highest score possible. In every move, pick one (A) or more moves (up to D) as possible next moves. Then, press "guess". If one of your guesses is correct, you earn points. The less options you pick, the most points you get if you are correct.

You can share your total score with your friends every {{showScoreFrequency}} moves or at the end of the game.

Have fun!
